International architecture and design practice founded by [[Zaha-Hadid]] in London. Following Hadid's death in 2016, the practice has been led by Patrik Schumacher. ZHA is one of the world's most prominent firms, known for large-scale civic and cultural projects characterized by flowing, algorithmically generated forms.

The practice is closely associated with [[parametric-design]] — a methodology developed in-house and theorized extensively by Schumacher, who has argued that parametricism constitutes the dominant new architectural style of the twenty-first century, a claim that remains contested.
